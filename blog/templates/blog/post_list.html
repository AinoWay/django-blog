The Way Everlasting

"There is still one greater mystery to be revealed. It is the discovery that deeper than at-one-ment lies comm-union, love come full in the act of giving itself away. The non-dualism of the Western metaphysical stream is a flowing unity, a ”not-one, not-two, but both-one-and-two”, in which the continuous exchange of twoness and oneness and the dance of self-giving, love captures the very dynamism of the divine life itself. To discover myself as a divine being is certainly a spiritual attainment, but to discover myself as the divine beloved is to discover something even more intimate and profound about the hidden treasure that God longs to make known."
